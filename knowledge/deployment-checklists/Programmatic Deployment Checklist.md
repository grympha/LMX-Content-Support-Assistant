-  Android 11+
    
-  WebView 120+
    
-  Hardware acceleration enabled
    

## Inventory Requirements

-  Inventory active
    
-  SSP integration active
    
-  Deal active

-  Creative approved
    
-  Budget available
    
-  Targeting verified

-  Impressions generated
    
-  Creative displayed successfully
