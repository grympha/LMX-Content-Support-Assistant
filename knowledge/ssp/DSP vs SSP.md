# DSP vs SSP

## DSP

Demand Side Platform

Used by advertisers and agencies to buy advertising inventory.

Examples:

- MAX DSP
    
- DV360
    

## SSP

Supply Side Platform

Used by media owners to sell advertising inventory.

Examples:

- LMX SSP
    
- VIOOH Supply
    

## Workflow

Advertiser  
↓  
DSP  
↓  
SSP  
↓  
CMS  
↓  
Device
