# Deal Management

## What is a Deal?

A Deal is an agreement that allows buyers to purchase inventory programmatically.

## Deal Types

### Open Auction

Available to all buyers.

### Private Marketplace (PMP)

Restricted access.

### Preferred Deal

Negotiated pricing.

### Programmatic Guaranteed

Guaranteed inventory delivery.
