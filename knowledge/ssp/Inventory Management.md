# Inventory Management

## What is Inventory?

Inventory represents advertising assets available for programmatic sales.

Examples:

- Digital Billboards
    
- Taxi Screens
    
- Bus Screens
    
- Train Screens
    

## Inventory Information

- Inventory Name
    
- Resolution
    
- Location
    
- Availability
    
- Media Type
    

## Best Practices

- Maintain accurate inventory information.
    
- Verify screen specifications.
    
- Monitor inventory health.
