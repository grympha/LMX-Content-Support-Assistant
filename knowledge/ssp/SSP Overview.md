# SSP Overview

## What is SSP?

SSP (Supply Side Platform) is a platform used by media owners to make advertising inventory available for programmatic buying.

## Purpose

The SSP connects advertising inventory with demand sources such as DSPs.

## Key Functions

- Inventory Management
    
- Deal Management
    
- Yield Optimization
    
- Programmatic Selling
