# Black Screen

## Symptoms

- Black screen displayed
- No content playback
- Device online in CMS

## Possible Causes

- Unsupported content format
- Resolution mismatch
- Device player stopped
- Internet unavailable

## Basic Troubleshooting

1. Verify device is online.
2. Check playlist assignment.
3. Verify content format.
4. Restart player application.
5. Confirm screen resolution.
