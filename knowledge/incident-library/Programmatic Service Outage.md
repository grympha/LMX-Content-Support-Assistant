# Programmatic Service Outage

## Incident Summary

Programmatic content delivery was interrupted.

## Impact

- VAST content not displayed
    
- Programmatic impressions affected
    

## Symptoms

- Empty ad slots
    
- Fallback content displayed
    
- Reduced programmatic delivery
    

## Investigation

Integration dependencies reviewed.

## Root Cause

Programmatic service interruption.

## Resolution

- Service restored
    
- Connectivity verified
    
- Campaign delivery monitored
    

## Lessons Learned

- Improve monitoring of programmatic integrations.
    
- Establish incident communication procedures.
