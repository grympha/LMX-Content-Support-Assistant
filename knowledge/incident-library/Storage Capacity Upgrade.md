# Storage Capacity Upgrade

## Incident Summary

Storage utilization reached critical levels.

## Impact

- Deployment delays
    
- Content processing issues
    
- Increased operational risk
    

## Investigation

Storage utilization exceeded recommended thresholds.

## Root Cause

Rapid growth of media assets and historical records.

## Resolution

Storage expanded:

- 8 GB → 20 GB
    

## Result

- Improved storage availability
    
- Reduced deployment risk
    

## Lessons Learned

- Monitor storage utilization continuously.
    
- Plan upgrades proactively.
