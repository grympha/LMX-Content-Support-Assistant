# Scheduling Content

## What is Scheduling?

Scheduling controls when content is displayed on devices.

## Scheduling Types

### Basic Schedule

Single date range.

### Advanced Schedule

Multiple date ranges and conditions.

### Daypart Schedule

Specific time periods.

### Daily Spot Schedule

Specific number of plays per day.

### Bundle Schedule

Multiple content packages.

## Best Practices

- Verify schedule dates.
    
- Verify assigned locations.
    
- Verify assigned devices.
    
- Confirm timezone settings.
