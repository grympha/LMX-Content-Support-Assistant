# Locations

## What is a Location?

A Location represents a physical site where screens are installed.

Examples:

- Shopping Mall
    
- Airport
    
- Transit Station
    
- Retail Outlet
    

## Purpose

Locations help organize:

- Devices
    
- Playlists
    
- Reporting
    
- Scheduling
    

## Best Practices

- Use actual site names.
    
- Include city or region when necessary.
    
- Maintain consistent naming standards.
