# Networks

## What is a Network?

A Network is the highest-level grouping structure within LMX Content.

Networks are typically used to organize screens by:

- Client
    
- Region
    
- Country
    
- Business Unit
    

## Example

Moving Walls Malaysia  
├── Kuala Lumpur  
├── Johor  
└── Penang

## Best Practices

- Use clear naming conventions.
    
- Separate clients into different networks.
    
- Avoid duplicate network names.
