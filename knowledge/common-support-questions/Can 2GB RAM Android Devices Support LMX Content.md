# Can 2GB RAM Android Devices Support LMX Content?

## Answer

Yes, but performance may be limited depending on content complexity.

## Recommended Usage

Suitable for:

- Images
    
- Basic videos
    
- Simple schedules
    

Not recommended for:

- Programmatic content
    
- Multiple zones
    
- Heavy HTML content
    

## Recommendation

4GB RAM or higher is recommended for production environments.
