# Can 2GB RAM Android Devices Support LMX Content?

## Answer

2GB RAM Android devices can support basic image and video playback only. They are not recommended for HTML, VAST, programmatic, or multi-zone deployments.

## Recommended Usage

Suitable for:

- Images
    
- Basic MP4 videos
    
- Simple schedules
    

Not recommended for:

- HTML content
    
- VAST campaigns
    
- Programmatic campaigns
    
- Multi-zone layouts
    

## Recommendation

Production recommendation: use at least 4GB RAM.
