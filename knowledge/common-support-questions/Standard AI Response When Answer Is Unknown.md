# Standard AI Response When Answer Is Unknown

If the assistant cannot find the answer within the knowledge base, use the following response:

"I couldn't find a specific answer for your question in the current knowledge base.

Please try rephrasing your question with more details, such as the device type, operating system, feature, or error message.

If you require further assistance, please contact our Support Helpdesk at [support@movingwalls.com](mailto:support@movingwalls.com) and provide any relevant screenshots or information."

## Usage

Use this response only when no relevant knowledge is found.
