# What Is The Recommended Android Specification?

## Recommended

### Operating System

Android 11 or higher

### RAM

4GB or higher

### Storage

32GB or higher

### Processor

Rockchip RK3568 or equivalent

### Network

Stable Ethernet connection preferred

## Programmatic Requirements

- Android 11+
    
- WebView 120+
