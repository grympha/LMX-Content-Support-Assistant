# Signals

## What are Signals?

Signals are audience and environmental data points used to improve campaign planning and optimization.

## Signal Categories

- Demographic
    
- Mobility
    
- Audience Behavior
    
- Location Intelligence
    

## Benefits

- Better targeting
    
- Improved audience reach
    
- Enhanced campaign performance
    

## Use Cases

- Audience selection
    
- Inventory selection
    
- Campaign optimization
