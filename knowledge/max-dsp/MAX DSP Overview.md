# MAX DSP Overview

## What is MAX DSP?

MAX DSP is Moving Walls' Demand Side Platform (DSP) used to plan, buy, and optimize Digital Out-of-Home (DOOH) advertising campaigns.

## Core Functions

- Campaign Planning
    
- Audience Targeting
    
- Inventory Selection
    
- Campaign Optimization
    
- Performance Reporting
    

## Campaign Modes

- Explore Mode
    
- Instant Mode
    
- Conventional Mode
