# Media Inventories

## What are Media Inventories?

Media Inventories represent available advertising assets within MAX DSP.

## Inventory Types

### Digital Billboards

Large format outdoor displays.

### Taxi Screens

Advertising screens installed in taxis.

### Bus Screens

Advertising screens installed in buses.

### Train Screens

Advertising screens installed in train systems.

### Street Furniture

Advertising assets located in public spaces.

## Benefits

- Audience reach
    
- Geographic coverage
    
- Campaign flexibility
