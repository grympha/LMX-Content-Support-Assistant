# New Campaign Missing

## Issue

Campaign was successfully created but not displayed on the intended screens.

## Symptoms

- Campaign active
    
- Devices online
    
- No playback observed
    

## Investigation

- Schedule verified
    
- Devices verified online
    
- Content verified available
    

## Root Cause

Location tag mismatch between campaign targeting and assigned inventory.

## Resolution

Updated location mapping and verified campaign assignment.

## Prevention

Always verify:

- Location Tags
    
- Inventory Assignment
    
- Campaign Scope
