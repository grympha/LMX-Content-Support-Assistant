# Resolution Mismatch - Optimum Exposure

## Issue

Content displayed incorrectly.

## Symptoms

- Cropped content
    
- Stretched content
    
- Black borders
    

## Investigation

Configured Resolution:

8550 x 2475

Actual Resolution:

2976 x 702

## Root Cause

Incorrect screen resolution configuration.

## Resolution

Updated layout and content to match actual screen resolution.
