# How to download LMX Content App
Here are the step how to download LMXC app directly from the platform
1. Login to your LMXC account
2. Go to the Dashboard page
3. Click on the “More” link at the bottom of the page in the “Release Information” section.on
- On the next page download the latest version of the Player based upon your device’s operating system
Notes**
SR = Software Render build — designed for devices without GPU acceleration, relying on CPU-based rendering instead.
