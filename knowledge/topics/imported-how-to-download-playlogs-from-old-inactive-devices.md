# How to Download Playlogs from Old/Inactive Devices
- Log in to the CMS platform.
- Go to the Device Manager tab.
- Select the old/inactive devices.
- Navigate to the Playlogs section.
- Select the Start Date and End Date, then click "Get Play Log"
(Make sure the selected dates fall within the period when the devices were active.)
- Once the playlogs are loaded, you can download the file.
