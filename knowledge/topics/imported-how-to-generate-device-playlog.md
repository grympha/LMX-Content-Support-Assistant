# How to generate Device Playlog
# Introduction
A Playlog is a record of all content played on a digital screen, including details like time, date, duration, and content ID. It helps track and verify content playback for reporting and auditing purposes.
## Step :
- Log in to the CMS – Go to https://cms.movingwalls.com/ and enter your credentials.
- Go to the “Device Manager” No 1 tab on the dashboard
- Select preferred device name and click Playlog No 2.
- On the playlog page select the specific date No 3.
- Once date select click “Generate” No 4.
- Once playlog generated, direct download by click download button
