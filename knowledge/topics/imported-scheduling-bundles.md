# Scheduling Bundles
A bundle is a group of content items (like images, videos, or HTML files) that are meant to be in the same playlist. Instead of scheduling each item one by one, you can use a bundle to schedule them all at once. This helps save time and keeps your campaign organized and consistent across different screens.
