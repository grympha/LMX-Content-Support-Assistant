# How to delete inactive devices
This guide explains how to safely remove inactive devices from the CMS.
## Warning
Before you start, please view what is the Impact of Deleting Devices in LMX Content Platform
### Important Note:
- Once deleted, the device will be permanently removed from the CMS.
- All historical playlogs tied to the device will no longer be accessible or downloadable.
- If you're unsure, consider deactivating the device as “archived” instead of deletion.
### Step-by-Step Instructions:
#### 1. Login to CMS
- Visit: https://cms.movingwalls.com
- Enter your Tenant Name, Username, and Password
Note: Login does not require an email address.
#### 2. Navigate to Device Manager
- Click on Device Manager from the main navigation menu.
- You’ll see a list of all registered devices and their current status.
- Use the search bar to locate the device(s) you intend to remove.
#### 3. Identify Inactive Devices
- Look for devices with:
Red/offline status
Last Seen date older than your expected threshold
- Apply filters if needed:
Status = Inactive or Offline
Last Seen > Older than X days
#### 4. Select the Device(s)
- Click the Status checkbox beside each inactive device you wish to delete.
- Double-check the device status before proceeding.
#### 5. Delete the Devices
- Click the Delete button
- A confirmation prompt will appear — click Confirm to proceed deleting the device and removing it and it's playlogs from the MW Content.
