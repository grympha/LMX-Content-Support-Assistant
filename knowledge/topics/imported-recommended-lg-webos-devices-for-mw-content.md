# Recommended LG webOS Devices for MW Content
### MW Content Requirement for WebOS
- OS: LG WebOS Signage 4.0.1
- Processor: α5 Gen5 AI / ARM Cortex-A53, Quad-Core 1.0 GHz
- System Type: 64-bit (recommended)
- RAM/ROM:
- Recommended: 4 GB RAM / 16 GB ROM
- Minimum: 2 GB RAM / 8 GB ROM
### Things to Note
- Always verify the webOS version before deployment. WebOS 4.0.1 and above is required.
- For optimal performance, devices with at least 4 GB RAM and 16 GB ROM are recommended.
- Devices running webOS 6.0 are backward compatible with many CMS platforms that support earlier versions like 4.x.
