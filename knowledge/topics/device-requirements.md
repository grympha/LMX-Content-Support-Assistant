# Device Requirements

Device requirements confirm whether OS, hardware, storage, RAM, browser/WebView, and media support are suitable for LMX Content.

## Android Requirements

- Android 11 and above.
- Quad-core CPU.
- Minimum 4GB RAM.
- Minimum 32GB or 64GB storage depending on deployment.
- Modern WebView, especially for VAST, URL, HTML, and programmatic content.

## Windows Requirements

- Windows 10 or Windows 11.
- Recommended Intel Core i5/i7.
- 8GB RAM recommended.
- Updated drivers and codecs.

## Programmatic Notes

- Windows is best for programmatic-heavy networks.
- Android 11 or 12 media players are second choice.
- Avoid Android 10 and below for programmatic formats.

## Common Mistakes

- Approving weak hardware.
- Ignoring WebView/browser version.
- Using unsupported OS versions.
- Assuming all smart displays support VAST or DV360.
