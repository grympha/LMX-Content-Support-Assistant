# Playlogs

Playlogs help confirm what content played on devices for reporting, audit, and validation.

## Steps

1. Open Playlogs or reports.
2. Select the correct date range.
3. Filter by network, location, device, campaign, or content where available.
4. Review playback records.
5. Export the report if needed.
6. Compare playlogs with schedule and device status.

## Important Notes

- Play Log should be enabled at network level.
- Date range matters.
- Offline devices may upload logs later after reconnecting.

## Common Mistakes

- Wrong date range.
- Expecting playlogs when content did not actually play.
- Checking before the device has uploaded logs.
- Play Log disabled.
