# How to Delete Content in the Storage
When the storage reaches its limit, it can cause operational issues such as:
- Inability to upload new content.
- Slower response time when navigating the Storage page.
To ensure smooth system performance and avoid disruptions, it's recommended to regularly manage and delete unused or outdated content.
## Steps to Delete Content from CMS Storage
- Navigate to the Main Storage:
From the dashboard, click on "Main Storage" in the left-hand navigation menu.
- Select Content to Delete:
You can choose a single content file or select multiple content files by using the checkboxes next to each item.
- Click Delete:
Once you've selected the content to be removed, click the "Delete" button at the top or bottom of the page.
A confirmation prompt may appear.
Confirm the deletion.
NOTE: Deleted content cannot be recovered. Ensure the content is not linked to any active campaign before deleting or it will fail for deleting.

## Best Practices
- Regularly audit your storage for unused or expired content.
- Coordinate with your team before deleting shared or frequently used files.
- Keep track of content size and plan periodic clean-ups to avoid reaching the storage limit.
