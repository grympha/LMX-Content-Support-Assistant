# System Requirements

## Windows

### Recommended

- Windows 10 or 11
    
- Intel Core i5/i7
    
- 8 GB RAM or higher
    
- 1 GB Graphics Card
    

### Minimum

- 4 GB RAM
    
- 64-bit Operating System
    

## Android

### Recommended

- Android 11+
    
- 4 GB RAM or higher
    
- Stable internet connection
    

### Minimum

- Android 7.0.1+
    
- 2 GB RAM (not recommended for heavy workloads)
    

## Programmatic Requirements

- Android 11+
    
- WebView 120+
    
- Hardware Acceleration Enabled
