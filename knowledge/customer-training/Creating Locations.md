# Creating Locations

## Purpose

Locations represent physical sites where devices are installed.

## Steps

1. Navigate to Locations.
    
2. Select Create Location.
    
3. Enter location details.
    
4. Assign network.
    
5. Save.
    

## Examples

- Airport
    
- Shopping Mall
    
- Retail Store
