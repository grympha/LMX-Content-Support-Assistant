# Playlogs and Reporting

## Purpose

Playlogs provide playback history and campaign verification.

## Report Types

### General Playlogs

Up to 30 days including current day.

### Device Playlogs

Historical playback records.

## Common Uses

- Proof of Play
    
- Campaign Verification
    
- Troubleshooting
