# Introduction to LMX Content

## What is LMX Content?

LMX Content is a Digital Signage Content Management System (CMS) that allows users to manage, schedule, deploy, and monitor content across multiple screens.

## Key Functions

- Content Management
    
- Device Management
    
- Scheduling
    
- Reporting
    
- Programmatic Content Delivery
    

## Main Components

- Networks
    
- Locations
    
- Devices
    
- Playlists
    
- Layouts
    
- Schedules
