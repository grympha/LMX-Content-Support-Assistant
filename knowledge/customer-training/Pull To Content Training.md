# Pull To Content Training

## Purpose

Pull To Content allows content to be dynamically assigned based on inventory mapping.

## Requirements

- Supported player version
    
- Device paired
    
- Inventory created
    
- Pull To Content enabled
    

## Workflow

Inventory  
↓  
Content Assignment  
↓  
Device Mapping  
↓  
Playback

## Validation

- Inventory mapped
    
- Content visible
    
- Device online
